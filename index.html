Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy Thai Delight

Ingredients, Preparation, and Cooking Tips

Tom Yum Goong

It is a popular Thai soup, renowned for its succulent taste and a veriety of rich flavors. It is often served with a side of steamed rice, sometimes with a dollop of chili paste and a splash of lime juice.
It is also packed with healthy ingredients such as lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, shallots, and cilantro leaves.

"Tom Yum Goong: the secret factors that make up the Tom Yum Goong delicious and irresistible are - sweet, sour, salty, and spicy - Chef Somchai "

Ingredients

  1. Lemongrass: 2 stalks
  2. Basil: 1 cup
  3. Kaffir Lime Leaves: 3 leaves
  4. Shrimp: 500 grams
  5. Fish Sauce: 2 tablespoons
  6. Lime Juice: 1 cup
  7. Water: 3 Cups
  8. Chili Paste: 2 Spoons

Preparation

  1. Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
  2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.
  3. Stir in shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Season with fish sauce, lime juice, and chili paste.
  5. Garnish with Thai basil and serve hot.

Cooking Tips:

  • 1/4cup of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor.
  • 1 tablespoon of chili paste gives the soup its signature heat.
